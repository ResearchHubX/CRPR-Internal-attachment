About Us

The CRPR is a comprehensive database that serves as a hub for information on research projects in artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, software engineering, data science, and more. Our platform facilitates easy access to project details, resources, outcomes, and collaborative opportunities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster transparency, knowledge sharing, and interdisciplinary collaboration within the Department of Computing. We aim to support researchers, students, and stakeholders by providing a robust repository of information and resources.

Our Plan

We plan to continuously update and expand our repository with the latest research projects and findings. Our goal is to include more domains and increase user engagement through interactive features and updated content.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading resource for computing research, contributing to academic excellence and innovation. We strive to be a valuable asset for prospective students, funding agencies, and industry partners looking to engage with cutting-edge research.
